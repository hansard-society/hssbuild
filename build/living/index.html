Chapter at King's Cross

A new development in one of London's most exciting and centrally located districts, Chapter at King's Cross is equipped with a full range of modern utilities, as well as sports facilities and social spaces that will make your stay in London comfortable, fun and energising.

Chapter is surrounded by an abundance of amenities and entertainment venues, including restaurants, cafes, supermarkets, retail outlets, theatres and music venues, the University of London campus and some of the world's best museums.
